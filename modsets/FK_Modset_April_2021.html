Arma 3 Mods

How to use this file:

  1. Open your Launcher.
  2. Click on "Mods" on the left hand side.
  3. Click on "More" at the top of the screen.
  4. Click on "List of Mods from file".
  5. Navigate to where you downloaded the preset to.
  6. Hit "OK".

And you can now add your own whitelisted clientside mods, and join us in our missions.
